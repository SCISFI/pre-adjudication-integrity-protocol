# Week 15 — No More Private Worlds

## Orientation

This week is about private worlds.

The goal is to identify the hidden spaces, routines, emotions, fantasies, accounts, stories, and behaviors that allowed a separate life to exist outside accountability.

## Core Teaching

A private world is more than a secret.

It is an internal or external space where a man lives by different rules.

In the public world, he may be husband, father, employee, church member, professional, helper, provider, or respected man.

In the private world, he may become entitled, hidden, sexually compulsive, resentful, fantasy-driven, emotionally numb, objectifying, dishonest, or detached from consequences.

The danger of a private world is that it creates a split self.

One part of the man wants integrity. Another part protects escape.

One part wants trust. Another part keeps secrets.

One part wants recovery. Another part keeps a door open.

Private worlds may include:

- secret accounts
- hidden routines
- private browser patterns
- fantasy rituals
- undisclosed resentments
- secret spending
- emotional isolation
- compartmentalized friendships
- hidden devices
- stories no one is allowed to question
- shame that is never spoken
- grief that becomes sexual escape

Recovery requires integration. The man must become one person again.

## Reality Check

You cannot build integrity while protecting a private world.

You cannot ask others to trust you while keeping hidden doors open.

You cannot become honest while preserving a version of yourself that no one is allowed to know.

The goal is not public humiliation. The goal is to stop living divided.

## Relationship / Family Reflection

If you have a spouse or partner, recognize that betrayal often includes the discovery that there was a private world she did not know existed. Do not demand that she see you as integrated before you have practiced integration over time.

If you are single or not partnered, consider how private worlds have weakened your ability to be known, trusted, and emotionally available.

## Therapist Prompt
