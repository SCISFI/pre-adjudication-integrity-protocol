# Week 4 — Who Belongs Where

## Orientation

This week is about role clarity.

In legal and relational crisis, men often put the wrong information in the wrong place or expect the wrong person to meet the wrong need. Recovery requires knowing who belongs where.

## Core Teaching

Different people have different roles.

Your attorney handles legal questions.

Your licensed therapist or supervised clinical provider handles clinical material.

Your clinical supervisor, when involved, supports treatment oversight.

Your spouse or partner is not your therapist, probation officer, emotional regulator, or recovery manager.

Your pastor, sponsor, mentor, or trusted support person may help you stay connected and honest, but they should not receive details that belong in therapy or legal counsel.

The court, case workers, evaluators, and legal professionals have defined roles. Do not use this app to rehearse what to tell them.

Role confusion creates harm. It can increase legal risk, deepen relational damage, overwhelm the wrong people, and give the appearance of accountability without real responsibility.

## Reality Check

Do not make your spouse carry your panic.

Do not make your attorney your therapist.

Do not make your therapist your legal strategist.

Do not make this app your confession record.

Do not make a support person responsible for information they are not qualified to hold.

Integrity includes putting the right truth in the right place with the right person.

## Legal and Clinical Boundary Reminder

Do not enter offense details, victim names, investigative facts, illegal content descriptions, police facts, or legal strategy. Use this space for recovery reflection, accountability, boundaries, emotions, and next right actions. Discuss legal details with your attorney and clinical details with your licensed therapist or supervised clinical provider.

If you are single or not partnered, identify one person you may be overusing, avoiding, or confusing with a role they should not hold.

- What belongs with my therapist?

- What does not belong in this app?
- What have I been trying to place on my spouse/partner or family?
