# MVP User Stories v1

## Participant Stories

### Onboarding

As a participant, I need to acknowledge the purpose and limits of the program before I begin so that I understand this is not therapy, legal advice, crisis care, or a legal-outcome tool.

Acceptance criteria:

- all required acknowledgments are displayed
- participant must actively check/attest before continuing
- participant must acknowledge licensed therapist/supervised provider involvement
- participant must acknowledge no-offense-detail policy

### Relationship Status

As a participant, I need to identify my relationship status so that relationship/family prompts can be adapted appropriately.

Options:

- married
- separated
- divorced
- cohabitating
- dating
- single
- no active partner

### Weekly Module

As a participant, I need to view my current weekly module and complete the reflection so I can practice structured accountability.

Acceptance criteria:

- Week 1–4 visible for MVP
- Week 5–24 hidden from initial test users
- legal/clinical boundary reminder appears
- reflection questions appear
- weekly commitment field appears
- completion checkbox is required

### Daily Check-In

As a participant, I need to complete a daily check-in that helps me notice vulnerability without being scored.

Acceptance criteria:

- HALT-BS options available
- sleep question available
- movement question available
- isolation question available
- strongest emotion available
- secrecy/escape vulnerability question available
- boundary question available
- support contact question available
- therapist follow-up question available
- daily attestation required

### AI Feedback

As a participant, I receive brief AI feedback after submitting weekly reflection or daily check-in.

Acceptance criteria:

- feedback is direct and non-shaming
- no legal advice
- no counseling advice
- no risk score
- no relapse prediction
- legal/clinical redirects appear when needed

## Clinician Stories

### Clinician Dashboard

As a clinician, I need to view assigned participants so I can review their structured accountability work.

Acceptance criteria:

- participant list visible
- current week visible
- last activity visible
- submitted/not submitted visible
- Needs Attention visible to clinician only

### Participant Detail

As a clinician, I need to view a participant’s weekly and daily submissions.

Acceptance criteria:

- weekly submission content visible
- daily check-in content visible
- AI feedback visible
- no score or risk interpretation appears

### Participation Summary

As a clinician/admin, I need to generate a participation summary that documents activity only.

Acceptance criteria:

- includes enrollment date
- includes weeks completed
- includes daily check-in count/date range
- includes last activity date
- includes required disclaimer
- excludes reflection content, AI feedback, offense details, risk opinions, and legal recommendations
