# App Build Specification v1

The app must follow:

- `docs/track-charter-v1.md`
- `docs/weekly-content-template-v1.md`
- `docs/daily-check-in-spec-v1.md`
- `docs/ai-feedback-guardrails-v1.md`
- `docs/participation-summary-rules-v1.md`
- `docs/participant-dashboard-spec-v1.md`
- `docs/clinician-dashboard-spec-v1.md`

## App Identity

Participant-facing name:

**The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol**

Subtitle:

**A recovery-focused accountability program for men facing serious sexual behavior consequences**

## Required MVP Features

The MVP must include:

1. participant onboarding acknowledgments
2. relationship status intake
3. weekly module display
4. weekly reflection form
5. weekly integrity commitment
6. completion checkbox
7. daily check-in form
8. AI feedback for weekly reflection and daily check-in
9. clinician dashboard
10. participant detail view
11. Needs Attention flag
12. participation summary prototype
13. no-offense-detail warnings
14. crisis language on onboarding and early weeks

## Explicitly Out of Scope for MVP

Do not implement:

- messaging
- notifications
- push alerts
- risk scoring
- relapse prediction
- legal-risk labels
- compliance percentages
- spouse/partner access
- attorney access portal
- court portal
- exports beyond authorized participation summary
- participant-to-clinician messaging
- automated escalation
- automated intervention
- AI clinical interpretation
- AI legal advice
- AI summaries for court

## Core Design Rule

The app holds structure. People hold meaning.
