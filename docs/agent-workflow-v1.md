# Agent Workflow v1

## Purpose

Multiple agents may work in parallel only when tasks are non-dependent and governed by the approved Track Charter.

The Track Charter controls meaning. The tracker controls execution.

## Approved Work Lanes

### Lane 1 — Curriculum

Works on weekly curriculum, workbook alignment, reflection language, and relationship/family adaptation.

Must follow the no-offense-detail policy.

### Lane 2 — App Shell

Works on routing, dashboards, forms, layout, data model, and user flows.

Must not create risk scoring, messaging, notifications, or legal/clinical interpretation.

### Lane 3 — AI Feedback

Works on prompts, prompt tests, guardrails, legal/clinical redirects, and AI output review.

Must not generate therapy, legal advice, court summaries, or risk statements.

### Lane 4 — Professional Materials

Works on attorney-facing overview, participation summary templates, release language, and referral documents.

Must not make legal outcome promises.

### Lane 5 — Testing / QA

Works on test plans, checklists, safety review, and user feedback collection.

Must not interpret clinical outcomes.

## Branch Naming

Use descriptive branches:

- `docs/...`
- `curriculum/...`
- `app/...`
- `ai/...`
- `testing/...`
- `professional-materials/...`

## Review Rule

Before merge, every change must be checked against:

- no offense-detail collection
- no legal advice
- no therapy replacement
- no forensic evaluation
- no risk scoring
- no court-outcome promises
- no spouse/partner pressure
- participation summaries document activity only

## Human Decision Required

Stop and ask Dr. Taylor if a proposed change affects:

- clinical ethics
- legal exposure
- user safety
- public positioning
- major architecture
- costs/subscriptions/deployment
- approved Charter language
- scope expansion beyond v1
