# Participant-Facing Overview v1
## The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol

The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol is a structured accountability and recovery-support program for men facing serious sexual behavior consequences before final legal resolution.

This program is not designed to help you avoid consequences.

It is designed to help you become honest, stable, accountable, treatment-engaged, and willing to do the work required of a man whose sexual behavior has brought serious consequences into his life.

## What This Program Helps You Work On

This program helps you reflect on:

- honesty
- secrecy
- shame
- minimization
- emotional avoidance
- adult pornography patterns
- escalation
- digital boundaries
- isolation
- accountability
- spouse/partner and family impact
- weekly integrity commitments
- what needs to be brought to your licensed therapist or supervised clinical provider

## What This Program Is Not

This program is not therapy.

It is not legal advice.

It is not a forensic evaluation.

It is not a psychosexual evaluation.

It is not a risk assessment.

It is not crisis care.

It is not a guarantee of any legal outcome.

It does not replace your attorney, your licensed therapist, your supervised clinical provider, or emergency support.

## Important Boundary

Do not enter offense details, victim names, investigative facts, illegal content descriptions, police facts, or legal strategy into this app.

Use this program for recovery reflection, accountability, boundaries, emotions, and next right actions.

Bring legal questions to your attorney.

Bring clinical details, offense-related concerns, emotional instability, safety concerns, and deeper recovery work to your licensed therapist or supervised clinical provider.

## The Right Motivation

Do not use this program to impress a court, attorney, spouse, case worker, solicitor, or judge.

The right posture is:

> I am doing this because integrity requires it, whether or not it helps my legal situation.
