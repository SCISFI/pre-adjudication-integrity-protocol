# Crisis Language v1

## Required Crisis Statement

Use this language during onboarding, Week 1, and Week 2:

> This program is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or believe you may harm yourself or someone else, call 911 or 988 now. Do not remain alone. Contact your licensed therapist, attorney-approved support person, sponsor, pastor, or emergency contact.

## AI Crisis Redirect

If a participant references suicidal thoughts, self-harm, danger to self, danger to others, or inability to stay safe, AI feedback must include:

> If you are in immediate danger or believe you may harm yourself or someone else, call 911 or 988 now. Do not remain alone. Contact your licensed therapist, attorney-approved support person, sponsor, pastor, or emergency contact.

## Boundary

The app must not attempt to counsel a crisis beyond crisis referral language.

The app must not imply that completing a reflection, receiving AI feedback, or checking in daily is sufficient crisis care.

## Dashboard Boundary

Crisis language should not generate automated legal-risk or clinical-risk scoring. Crisis-related content must prompt human clinical review according to clinical policy, not algorithmic interpretation.
