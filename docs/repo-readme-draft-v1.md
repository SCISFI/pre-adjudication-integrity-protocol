# The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol

A specialized Integrity Protocol program for men facing serious sexual behavior consequences before final legal resolution.

## Purpose

The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol provides structured recovery reflection, daily check-ins, accountability support, and clinician visibility for men who voluntarily want help and are engaged with licensed clinical care or supervised clinical providers.

## Core Guardrails

The program must not:

- collect offense details
- provide legal advice
- replace therapy
- perform forensic evaluation
- score risk
- predict relapse
- imply legal benefit
- pressure spouse/partner reconciliation
- generate court-facing clinical conclusions

The program may:

- support structured accountability
- provide weekly recovery reflection
- support daily self-attestation
- encourage licensed therapy engagement
- redirect legal matters to attorneys
- document participation facts when authorized
