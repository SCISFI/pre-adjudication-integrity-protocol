# Attorney-Facing Overview v1
## The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol

**A recovery-focused accountability program for men facing serious sexual behavior consequences**

The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol is a structured accountability and recovery-support program for men facing unresolved legal, relational, family, or safety consequences related to problematic sexual behavior.

The program is designed for men who voluntarily want help, are willing to engage in structured weekly reflection, and are working with a licensed therapist or supervised graduate-level mental health intern.

## Program Focus

The protocol supports work in the following areas:

- stabilization after exposure, accusation, arrest, or removal from the home
- honesty, accountability, and emotional grounding
- adult pornography patterns, secrecy, fantasy, escalation, and minimization
- digital boundaries and environmental containment
- relational and family impact
- spouse/partner response without pressure for reconciliation
- continued engagement with licensed clinical care
- responsible participation while legal matters remain unresolved

## What the Program Does Not Do

The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol does not provide legal advice, determine guilt or innocence, assess legal responsibility, evaluate forensic risk, provide psychosexual evaluation, make clinical treatment determinations, or recommend adjudication, sentencing, probation, registration, custody, or contact outcomes.

The program does not collect offense narratives, victim names, investigative details, legal strategy, or attorney-client privileged information.

## Documentation

When authorized by the participant, the program may provide a Participation Summary documenting structured activity only, such as enrollment date, weeks completed, completion attestations, daily check-in counts, and last activity date.

The Participation Summary does not include reflection content, AI feedback, offense details, clinical interpretations, risk opinions, or legal recommendations.

## Required Disclaimer

Any Participation Summary should include:

> This summary documents participation only. It is not a forensic evaluation, psychosexual evaluation, risk assessment, legal opinion, clinical treatment summary, treatment discharge summary, or sentencing recommendation. It does not determine guilt or innocence, verify truthfulness, assess safety, establish rehabilitation, or recommend any legal outcome.

## Referral Fit

This program may be appropriate for adult men who are facing serious sexual behavior consequences and who want structured recovery accountability while legal, relational, or family circumstances remain unresolved.

It is not appropriate as a substitute for legal counsel, licensed therapy, crisis care, forensic evaluation, or mandated treatment.
