# Onboarding Acknowledgments v1

Each participant must acknowledge these statements before beginning The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol.

## Required Acknowledgments

### 1. Program Purpose

I understand that The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol is a structured accountability and recovery-support program for men facing serious sexual behavior consequences.

### 2. Not Therapy

I understand that this program is not therapy, counseling, crisis care, clinical treatment, forensic evaluation, psychosexual evaluation, or mandated treatment.

### 3. Licensed Therapist / Supervised Provider Requirement

I agree to work with a licensed therapist or supervised graduate-level mental health intern where clinical details, offense-related issues, risk-related concerns, emotional instability, and deeper recovery work can be addressed appropriately.

### 4. Not Legal Advice

I understand that this program does not provide legal advice. I will bring legal questions, legal strategy, investigative matters, and court-related concerns to my attorney.

### 5. No Offense Details in the App

I agree not to enter offense details, victim names, child names, investigative facts, illegal content descriptions, police facts, confession narratives, legal strategy, or attorney-client privileged information into this app.

### 6. Crisis Support

I understand that this program is not a crisis service. If I am in immediate danger or believe I may harm myself or someone else, I will call 911 or 988 and contact appropriate emergency or professional support.

### 7. No Legal Outcome Promise

I understand that participation in this program does not promise, imply, or guarantee any legal benefit, reduced consequence, court outcome, sentencing result, custody/contact outcome, or professional opinion about my safety or rehabilitation.

### 8. Participation Summary Limits

I understand that any Participation Summary documents activity only. It does not include reflection content, AI feedback, offense details, clinical interpretation, risk opinion, or legal recommendation.

### 9. Honest Completion

I understand that weekly and daily completion attestations are self-attestations of honesty, not scores, rewards, punishments, or proof of change.

### 10. Relationship Boundary

I understand that this program is not designed to pressure my spouse/partner, family, or others toward forgiveness, reconciliation, reassurance, or trust.

## Final Required Attestation

> I understand the purpose and limits of The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol. I agree to use this program for structured recovery reflection, accountability, and integrity work while bringing legal matters to my attorney and clinical matters to my licensed therapist or supervised clinical provider.
