# Workbook Outline v1
## The Pre-Adjudication Integrity Protocol

## Format

A companion workbook should align with the app and provide an offline option for clients who cannot use the internet.

## Required Front Matter

- Title page
- Disclaimer
- Crisis support statement
- No-offense-detail warning
- Licensed therapist / supervised provider requirement
- How to use this workbook
- Completion attestation explanation

## Core Disclaimer

The workbook is not therapy, legal advice, forensic evaluation, psychosexual evaluation, risk assessment, crisis care, treatment discharge, or sentencing recommendation.

Participants should not write offense details, victim names, investigative facts, legal strategy, or attorney-client privileged information in the workbook.

## Structure

Each week should include:

1. Week title
2. Orientation
3. Core teaching
4. Reality check
5. Legal and clinical boundary reminder
6. Reflection lines
7. Relationship/family reflection
8. Therapist prompt
9. Weekly integrity commitment
10. Completion attestation checkbox

## Weekly Sections

Use the approved 24-week curriculum map.

## Writing Space

Because the workbook may be printed, provide lined writing areas rather than fillable boxes.

## Participation Boundary

The workbook may support therapy or accountability, but completion of workbook pages does not prove recovery, safety, rehabilitation, legal compliance, or treatment completion.

## Alignment

The workbook should visually and structurally align with existing Integrity Protocol workbook materials while maintaining a more sober and legally cautious tone.
